About Me

I am a Research Fellow at CNRS@CREATE in Singapore since August 2023 working with Prof. Victor Magron and Prof. Jean-Bernard Lasserre. I received my doctor's degree of mathematics under the supervision of Prof. Lihong Zhi at AMSS-CAS in July 2023.

I'm working on robust polynomial optimization and its applications in neural network verification.

My current postdoctoral fellowship will conclude in August 2024. I am actively seeking new postdoctoral opportunities to continue my research in polynomial optimization and neural network verification.
